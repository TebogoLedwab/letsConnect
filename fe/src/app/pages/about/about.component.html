The Connekt Application

  • Home

    The home page is the first page you see right after loging into Connekt.It has the list of all the interns that have registered into our applications.You get to see the intern's name and the points they have accummulated by engaging with our system.

    +30 Points after logging in every after 30 days

  • Community

    Communty is a question and answer section.Here you can post any technical issues that you would like to be assisted in.By doing so you will accumulate (number of points).Other interns are then able to either like,dislike or comment on your post.

    +30 Points every after 30 days

  • Blog

    Blog is a page where anyone who has registered into the app gets to share any trending,latest tech news.By doing so you will accumulate (number of points).Other interns are only able to either like or dislike your post.

    +30 Points every after 30 days

  • Dashboard

    Dashboard is the user's profile,where they get to either add,delete or edit their personal information to help the company keep track of their employment statuses.

    +20 Points every after 30 days.

  • Profile

    This page is for a specific user's profile information.Everyone who uses Connekt is able to access this page,only by clicking on a specific intern from the intern's list.This will enable you to get more information about that specific intern.

    No points.

  • Admin Dashboard

    This page section is only accessed by the admin to get the applications stats.

    No points,Only viewed by admin.
